eset Review

ESET (an acronym for ‘Essential Security against Evolving Threats’) are a PC protection software company based out of Bratislava, Slovakia. Their main antivirus product, ESET NOD32 (NOD being an acronym for Nemocnica na Okraji Disku, or “Hospital at the end of the disk” in English) has a solid place in the history of antivirus products as it was one of the first antiviruses to ever be awarded the Virus Bulletin 100 Test Award in 1998, an award only given to antivirus products with algorithms able to find and correctly section 100% of the malware samples in a test. With almost 30 different products to choose from, it can be hard to know exactly what you are getting with ESET. Has their antivirus continued the quality performance from the early days of PC protection? Is it still one of the best in the market?

Reliability and Security

One measure ESET excels in their Reliability, NOD32 is one of the strongest antivirus products on the market thanks to the heuristic algorithms it employs. Heuristic algorithms (in this case) are the algorithms used by antivirus products to spot malware which they have not seen an example of before. Spotting an antivirus which the company has seen before is easy, ESET are considered the best in the industry for spotting malware no one has seen before. The product itself is very easy to use thanks to it’s simple UI. You can tell very easily if you are fully protected (or at least as protected as ESET can make you), how to do a scan and how to get help.

In section, ideally we’d go through how ESET make up for poor features through their brilliant support, but that’s not the case this time.
